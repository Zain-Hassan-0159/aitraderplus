    Privacy Policy

    Last Updated: [6/10/2024]

    Alkameida ST Solutions Corp. (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the ZiporBot platform. By accessing or using ZiporBot, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

    1. Information We Collect

    We collect different types of information to operate effectively, deliver our services, and improve our platform. This includes:

    • Personal Information: When you register an account with ZiporBot, we collect personal data such as your name, email address, and payment information, which may include credit card details.
    • Device and Connection Information: To ensure a seamless experience, we collect information about the devices you use to access ZiporBot, including IP address, device identifiers, operating system, and browser type.
    • Usage Information: We track your interactions with ZiporBot to understand how you use our services, which features you access, login details, and your trade history. This data helps us enhance and optimize user experience.
    • Transaction and Financial Information: We collect and store your trading activity on ZiporBot, such as trade entries and exits, for performance tracking, analytics, and reporting.

    We collect this information directly from you during account registration, interaction with our services, and through automated means like cookies and server logs.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to deliver, enhance, and secure our services, as well as to comply with legal obligations. Specifically, we may use your information for the following purposes:
    • To Provide and Operate Services: Your personal data enables us to set up your account, process transactions, and carry out automated trades on your behalf.
    • To Enhance User Experience: By analyzing usage patterns and feedback, we continually improve ZiporBot’s functionality, features, and performance.
    • To Communicate with You: We use your contact information to send account-related updates, respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and share important notices about changes to our services or policies.
    • For Security and Compliance: To protect our users, we monitor accounts and activities for suspicious or fraudulent behavior. This helps us ensure a safe environment and comply with regulatory requirements.
    • To Comply with Legal Requirements: We may process your data to meet regulatory obligations such as anti-money laundering (AML) and tax reporting. Where necessary, we may also process information for compliance with applicable laws.

    3. Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. However, there are certain situations where we may share your data:
    • With Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party vendors to help us deliver our services. These providers assist with activities such as payment processing, infrastructure support, security monitoring, and data analysis. All service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and may only use your data to perform specific tasks on our behalf.
    • With Partner Brokers: ZiporBot may integrate with third-party brokers for executing trades. If you choose to connect with a broker through ZiporBot, we may share your transaction data to facilitate trading activities.
    • For Legal and Regulatory Purposes: We may disclose your personal information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena, or to comply with regulatory requirements. We may also disclose information to protect our rights, investigate violations of our Terms of Service, or respond to claims of illegal or harmful activities.
    • In the Event of Business Transfers: If we undergo a merger, acquisition, sale, or reorganization, your information may be transferred as part of the assets of Alkameida ST Solutions Corp. In such cases, we will notify you and update this policy if your data will be used in a manner inconsistent with its original purpose.

    4. Security of Your Information

    We take your privacy and security seriously. To protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, we implement a range of security measures, including:
    • Encryption: Sensitive information, such as payment data, is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
    • Access Controls: We restrict access to personal information to employees, contractors, and agents who need it to perform their jobs. All parties are bound by confidentiality obligations and may face disciplinary or legal actions if they violate these obligations.
    • Regular Security Audits: We routinely assess our security practices and infrastructure to identify vulnerabilities and improve protections.

    Despite our best efforts, please note that no security measures are entirely foolproof. We encourage you to use strong passwords and secure your devices to further protect your information.

    5. Data Retention

    We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal requirements, and resolve disputes. Specifically:
    • Active Account Period: As long as you maintain an active account with ZiporBot, we retain your information for account management and service delivery purposes.
    • Post-Account Closure: When you close your account, we may retain certain information as necessary to meet legal or regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Data that is no longer required is securely deleted or anonymized.
    • Legal Requirements: In compliance with legal and regulatory standards, some information may need to be retained for a specified period (e.g., financial transaction records) even after account closure.

    6. Your Rights

    Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data. These rights may include:
    • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
    • Right to Correction: If you believe your information is incorrect or incomplete, you have the right to request corrections.
    • Right to Deletion: Under certain conditions, you may request that we delete your personal data, although some information may be retained as required by law.
    • Right to Restriction: You may request limited processing of your data in specific situations, such as during a dispute over data accuracy.
    • Right to Data Portability: Where applicable, you can request your data in a portable format for transfer to another service.

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at legal@ziporalgoai.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request, and some requests may be subject to limitations or fees.

    7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    ZiporBot uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality and analyze user interactions. Cookies help us understand user behavior, optimize performance, and provide personalized services. The types of cookies we may use include:
    • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic site functions, such as account login and security.
    • Analytics Cookies: Gather data on usage patterns, which helps us improve ZiporBot’s performance.
    • Functionality Cookies: Enable additional features, such as remembering your preferences.

    You can modify your browser settings to manage cookie preferences. However, disabling cookies may impact the functionality of ZiporBot.

    8. Third-Party Links

    Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those websites, and we are not responsible for their content, privacy practices, or data handling. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit through links on ZiporBot.

    9. Children's Privacy

    ZiporBot is designed for users over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 18. If we discover that we have unintentionally collected personal information from a minor, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we may have collected information from a child, please contact us at legal@ziporalgoai.com.

    10. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our data practices or legal requirements. When significant updates are made, we will notify users by revising the “Last Updated” date and, if required, providing additional notice. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
